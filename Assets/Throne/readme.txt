This Asset pack contains model of a Throne. 

A great throne is always important for a king, is the place 
where you take decisions that define the future of his kingdom. 
The throne is essential for environments of fantasy and RPG games.

1 Textures are in .PNG file format (diffuse), resolution are 1024 x 1024.

They are all configured correctly for use with Unity,
just import into your project.
